Research Rigour & Open Science

Transparent and reproducible research practices are essential for scientific progress. I am committed to open science principles, from pre-registration to data sharing, ensuring that my work can be verified, replicated, and built upon by others.

Pre-Registration

All confirmatory studies are pre-registered before data collection, specifying hypotheses, methods, and analysis plans to prevent p-hacking and ensure transparent hypothesis testing.

Analysis code and computational environments are shared openly, allowing others to verify results and adapt methods for their own research questions.
