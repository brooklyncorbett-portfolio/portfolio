Why This Matters

The Central Limit Theorem explains why we can use normal distribution tools (z-tests, t-tests) even when our population data isn't normally distributed. Try this: Start with the bimodal distribution, set n = 30, and draw 100 samples. Watch two peaks transform into one bell curve!
