AI in Education

I create AI-powered learning tools grounded in cognitive and educational psychology. These tools combine evidence-based principles, data-informed design, and practical AI capabilities to support deeper student learning. They have been adapted across courses in psychology, education, nutrition science, and computer science at the University of Queensland.

This page showcases some of these tools and includes practical guides for educators wanting to design or adopt AI-supported learning experiences.
